"Build With" vs "Build For"

As practitioners we have found through our many years of experience that the best approach to building meaningful and sustainable momentum for your business is to work side-by-side with your team. We can help develop your team's self efficacy and capabilities for running your platform in the cloud.

Flexible Delivery

Do you need help designing and delivering your solution in the cloud?

Flexible Delivery is the ideal approach when the end design is a moving target. This iterative delivery approach allows for rapid proof-of-concept infrastructure development and affords the agility to pivot to and from different designs until your team lands on a solution that best fits your business needs.

Examples:

  • Data Center Migrations
  • Rapid Proof of Concepts
  • Pipeline Development

Fixed Delivery

Do you have a deadline for delivering a new solution in the cloud?

Fixed Delivery is the ideal approach when you have well-defined target and statement of work with fixed scope in the form of documented requirements and a timeline for delivery. This project-based approach allows you to projects that are well defined in their scope and timeline over to our team for rapid delivery.

Examples:

  • New AWS Platform as Code
  • CI/CD Integrations and Tooling
  • Custom DevOps Solutions
  • Architecture and Security Reviews
